PIST Sports: Empowering Student-Athletes


At PIST, we believe that sports and academics go hand-in-hand. Our sports programs foster teamwork, discipline, and physical well-being, complementing our students' academic pursuits.

  • Fitness center with cardio and strength training equipment
  • Outdoor fields for soccer, rugby, and cricket
