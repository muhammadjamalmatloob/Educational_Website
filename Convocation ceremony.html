CONVOCATION CEREMONY

Celebrating Academic Excellence: Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology Convocation Ceremony 2024


The Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology is proud to announce its Convocation Ceremony 2024, marking a significant milestone in the academic journey of our graduating students. This momentous occasion will bring together graduates, faculty members, parents, and distinguished guests to share in the joy and pride of this remarkable achievement. On 20 July/2024, we will gather at Pak Auditorium Complex to honor the outstanding accomplishments of our students.


A Tribute to Academic Excellence


The Convocation Ceremony is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and perseverance of our graduating students. Over the years, they have demonstrated exceptional academic prowess, innovative thinking, and a passion for learning. Our faculty members have played a pivotal role in shaping their minds and nurturing their talents. We take immense pride in their achievements and look forward to celebrating their success.


Keynote Address and Honorary Degrees


This year's Convocation Ceremony will feature a keynote address by Prof.M.Jamal, a renowned expert in Computer Science. We will also confer honorary degrees upon Prof.Ahmad, recognizing their outstanding contributions to Computer Science. These distinguished individuals will inspire our graduates to strive for excellence and make a positive impact in their chosen fields.


Celebrating Institutional Pride


The Convocation Ceremony is a celebration of institutional pride, showcasing the Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology's commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement. We invite all graduates, family members, faculty, staff, and well-wishers to join us for this momentous occasion. Your presence will make this day even more special for our graduating students.


Legacy of Excellence: Shaping Future Leaders


As we celebrate the achievements of our graduating students, we reflect on the Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology's legacy of excellence. For years, our institution has been nurturing talented individuals, equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to succeed in an increasingly complex world. Today's graduates join a distinguished alumni network, comprising leaders, innovators, and change-makers who have made a profound impact in their respective fields. We are confident that our graduates will carry forward this legacy, shaping the future of science, technology, and society.
