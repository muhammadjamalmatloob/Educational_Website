Health Facilities at PIST


Welcome to the PIST Health Facilities page, where we prioritize the health and well-being of our students, faculty, and staff. Our campus health services provide both basic and urgent care to ensure that your healthcare needs are met quickly and efficiently. Whether you require routine check-ups or immediate medical attention, our healthcare team is here to support you with expert care in a convenient, on-campus setting.


Our Basic Health Services include:


  • Primary Care: Annual physical exams, vaccinations, and wellness checks.
  • Mental Health Services: Counseling, stress management, and support for anxiety or depression.
  • Preventive Care: Health screenings, immunizations, and health education.
  • Laboratory Services: Basic lab tests and diagnostics, including blood work and urinalysis.

For more urgent health needs, our Urgent Care Services are available to handle situations that require immediate attention, including:


  • Acute Illnesses and Injuries: Treatment for flu, colds, sprains, and minor fractures.
  • Emergency Medical Services: Immediate care for sudden health issues that arise on campus.
  • Wound Care: Stitches, burns, and minor trauma treatments.
  • On-site Pharmacy: Quick access to prescriptions and over-the-counter medications.

With compassionate, skilled staff and a focus on student health, our health facilities offer timely, high-quality care to help you stay well and succeed academically. Whether it's routine care or an urgent medical concern, we are here to support your health every step of the way.
