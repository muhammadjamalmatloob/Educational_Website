SCHOLARSHIPS

Empowering Your Education


At Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology, we believe that financial constraints should not hinder your educational aspirations. Our scholarship programs aim to recognize and reward academic excellence, talent, and diversity.


Types of Scholarships


  • Merit-Based Scholarships
  • Need-Based Scholarships
  • Talent-Based Scholarships (Art, Music, Sports)
  • Diversity Scholarships
  • International Student Scholarships

Eligibility Criteria


  • Academic performance
  • Financial need
  • Community service
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Leadership skills

Application Process


  • Submit online application
  • Upload required documents
  • Review and selection by scholarship committee
  • Notification of award

Available Scholarships


  • HEC Need Based Scholarship
  • Honahaar Scholarship for Needy and Deserving Students
  • PIST Need Based Scholarship
  • Merit Scholarship for talented students
