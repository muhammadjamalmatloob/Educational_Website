CAREER FAIR

Unlock Your Future: Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology Career Fair 2024


The Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology is excited to announce its annual Career Fair 2024, a premier event connecting talented students and alumni with leading employers and innovators in the industry. On 15 May/2024 , join us at Iqbal auditorium to explore exciting career opportunities, network with professionals, and take the first step towards a successful future.


Connecting Talent with Opportunity


Our Career Fair offers a unique platform for students and alumni to interact with renowned companies, startups, and organizations from various sectors, including tech, engineering, healthcare. With over fifty participating companies, this event promises to provide unparalleled access to job and internship opportunities, career guidance, and industry insights.


Showcasing Your Skills


We invite students and alumni to showcase their skills, experience, and achievements to potential employers. Bring multiple copies of your resume, prepare thoughtful questions, and be ready to make a lasting impression. Our Career Services team will also provide guidance on resume-building, interview preparation, and job search strategies.


Industry Insights and Workshops


The Career Fair will also include industry insights and workshops, providing valuable information on emerging trends, required skills, and career paths. Renowned experts will share their experiences, offering practical advice on how to succeed in your chosen field.


Networking Opportunities


Our Career Fair offers extensive networking opportunities, allowing you to connect with peers, alumni, and professionals from various industries. Expand your network, build meaningful relationships, and stay updated on industry developments.
