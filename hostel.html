PIST Hostel Facilities: Your Home Away from Home


At PIST, we understand that a comfortable living environment is essential for academic success. Our hostel facilities provide a safe, convenient, and inclusive space for students to live, learn, and grow.


Hostel Features


  • - Spacious rooms with ensuite bathrooms
  • Fully furnished rooms with beds, study tables, and wardrobes
  • 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the hoste
  • Laundry facilities and washing machines
  • Common rooms with TVs, sofas, and recreational activities
  • On-site gym and fitness center
  • Hygienic and nutritious meal options in the cafeteria
