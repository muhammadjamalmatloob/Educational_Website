RESEARCH SEMINAR

Exploring Frontiers of Knowledge: Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology Research Seminar 2024


The Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology is proud to announce its Research Seminar 2024, a premier event showcasing cutting-edge research in science and technology. This year's seminar, themed "Innovations for a Sustainable Future," will bring together renowned experts, researchers, and scholars to share their groundbreaking work and explore solutions to pressing global challenges. On 24 July/2024, join us at KICS for a stimulating day of presentations, discussions, and networking.


Fostering Collaboration and Knowledge Sharing


The Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology Research Seminar 2024 aims to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers, academics, industry professionals, and policymakers. This event will provide a unique opportunity for participants to engage with experts, explore new research avenues, and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. We invite researchers, students, faculty members, and industry professionals to join us for this enriching experience.


Showcasing Research Excellence


The seminar will showcase research excellence from the Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology, highlighting the institution's commitment to innovation, academic rigor, and community engagement. Our researchers will present their work on various themes, including renewable energy, artificial intelligence, healthcare This showcase will demonstrate the institution's capacity for impactful research and its potential to drive positive change.


Empowering Young Researchers


The Pakistan Institute of Science and Technology Research Seminar 2024 is committed to empowering young researchers and providing a platform for them to share their ideas and research findings. We encourage students and early-career researchers to participate, present their work, and receive feedback from experienced professionals. This supportive environment will help nurture the next generation of researchers and innovators.


Interdisciplinary Approaches to Sustainable Development


This year's seminar will focus on interdisciplinary approaches to sustainable development, exploring the intersection of science, technology, and society. Participants will engage with experts from diverse fields, including environmental science, engineering, economics This holistic approach will facilitate a deeper understanding of complex challenges and foster innovative solutions.
