Transportation

University Transportation: Safe, Convenient, and Accessible


At PIST, we understand that transportation plays a vital role in ensuring students, faculty, and staff reach campus safely and efficiently. Our transportation services aim to provide a convenient, reliable, and sustainable commute.


Transportation Options


    1. University Shuttle Service

  • Routes connecting campus to nearby residential areas, public transportation hubs, and parking facilities
  • Frequency: Every 15-30 minutes
  • Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the hoste
  • Laundry facilities and washing machines
  • Schedule: Available online or through mobile app

    • 2. Campus Parking

  • Designated parking lots for students, faculty, and staff
  • Fee structure: Daily, monthly, and semester passes available

    • 3. Public Transportation Discounts

  • Partnerships with local transit authorities for reduced fares
  • Information: Available at the Transportation Office

    • 4. Ride-Sharing and Carpooling

  • Online platform for connecting riders and drivers
  • Benefits: Reduced fuel costs, parking, and environmental impact

    • 5. Bicycle-Friendly Campus

  • Rentals and repair services available
  • Designated bike lanes and storage facilities

Room Options

  • Double occupancy rooms
  • Triple occupancy rooms
  • - Suites with shared living areas

Sustainability Initiatives


    1. Electric and Hybrid Vehicles

  • Environmentally friendly shuttle buses

    • 2. Carpooling and Ride-Sharing Incentives

  • Discounts and rewards for participants

    • 3. Bike-to-Work Program

  • Encouraging alternative transportation methods
