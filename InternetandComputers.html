INTERNET AND COMPUTERS

Internet and Computers at PIST


At PIST, we provide students and faculty with high-speed internet and modern computing resources to enhance the academic experience. Our campus is equipped with a reliable, secure, and fast internet connection, ensuring that you can stay connected to essential online platforms and resources. Whether you're in the classroom, library, or at home, you can access the tools you need for learning, research, and communication.


Key Internet Features:


  • High-speed Wi-Fi across campus, including classrooms, dorms, and common areas
  • Secure access to academic databases, online journals, and learning management systems
  • Reliable connection for video conferencing, virtual classes, and collaborative projects

Our computer labs are equipped with up-to-date software and hardware to support a wide range of academic needs. Whether you're working on engineering projects, developing software, designing graphics, or conducting research, our labs provide the resources to succeed. Additionally, we offer remote desktop access so you can work from anywhere on or off-campus.


Key Computing Resources:


  • Modern workstations with specialized software (e.g., AutoCAD, Adobe Creative Suite, MATLAB)
  • 24/7 access to computer labs and study spaces
  • Remote access to university software and files from personal devices

To ensure a smooth and efficient experience, our IT support team is here to assist with any technical issues you may encounter. From troubleshooting to software installation, we’re dedicated to helping you make the most of the university’s digital resources.


IT Support Services:


  • On-campus helpdesk for technical assistance and troubleshooting
  • Software installation and system updates for students and faculty
  • Access to online tutorials, guides, and troubleshooting resources
