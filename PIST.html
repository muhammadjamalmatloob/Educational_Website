FACILITIES

Library

The University has a library where the students can read books and can gain knowledge in different fields.

Sports

The University has sports stadiums where the sports tournaments helds among the teams of different departments.

Transport

The University has the facility of transport at reasonable charges for the ease of students and faculty members.

Scholaships

The University provides merit and need based scholarships to needy and deserving students with stipend.

Hostels

The University has ample provisions for hostel accommodation. It has five halls of residence with accommodation for about 300 students.

Cafeterias

The University has many cafeterias where the students can get different eating and drinking items.

Inernet & Computers

The University has its own free wifi for the students. Computers are available for the use of students in labs.

Health Facilities

The University has its own heath service where the students can get medicine and other necessary things in emergency case.
