Welcome to PIST Cafeteria


Fuel your mind and body at our vibrant and diverse cafeteria, offering a wide range of delicious and nutritious options to satisfy every taste and dietary need.


Cafeteria Features


  • Spacious dining area with comfortable seating
  • Variety of cuisine stations (International, Asian, Italian, Vegetarian/Vegan)
  • Fresh fruit and salad bar
  • Made-to-order sandwiches and wraps
  • SHot and cold beverage stations (coffee, tea, juice)
  • Dessert and pastry corner

    • Menu Highlights


  • Designated parking lots for students, faculty, and staff
  • Breakfast options (7:00 am - 10:00 am)
  • Eggs, pancakes, waffles, oatmeal, and more
  • Lunch options (11:00 am - 2:00 pm)
  • Sandwiches, salads, soups, pasta, and international dishes
  • Dinner options (5:00 pm - 8:00 pm)
  • Grilled meats, seafood, vegetarian/vegan options, and daily specials
  • Snacks and refreshments (throughout the day)

    • Special Dietary Options


  • Vegetarian and vegan options
  • Gluten-free and lactose-free alternatives
  • Halal and kosher options
  • Food allergy accommodations (notify staff)

    • Cafeteria Services


  • Cashless payment system (campus card or mobile payments)
  • Meal plans and flexible spending accounts
  • Catering services for events and meetings
  • Nutrition counseling and education

    • Operating Hours


  • Monday - Thursday: 7:00 am - 8:00 pm
  • Friday: 7:00 am - 6:00 pm
  • Saturday - Sunday: 10:00 am - 6:00 pm
